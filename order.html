Delivery Details

Cocalabs delivers brownies directly to your door. Once you submit your order, we’ll send an email to you and to our kitchen at rannvijaypadhi@gmail.com with your order details.

You’ll receive a delivery window and a confirmation link. All deliveries are scheduled based on our midnight baking queue.

Delivery‑Only Kitchen

We don’t offer pickup or a physical storefront. Cocalabs is built around the idea of cozy brownies arriving when you need them, without you having to leave home.

If you’re planning a party, event, or late‑night study session, mention it in your notes and we’ll help you plan the right amount of brownies.

Once your order is submitted, check your email for delivery details.
